How To Save Money with Agile Octopus?

Agile Octopus offers a unique opportunity to save money on your energy bills by taking advantage of dynamic pricing. By shifting your energy usage to off-peak hours, you can significantly reduce your costs.

Schedule Your Appliance

One of the most effective ways to save money with Agile Octopus is by scheduling your energy-intensive appliances like washing machines, tumble dryers, and dishwashers to run during off-peak hours.

Using our price tracker, identify the periods when energy prices are lowest

Set a timer or use the delay function on your appliances to operate during these times.

By aligning your appliance usage with off-peak hours, you can significantly reduce your energy consumption and, consequently, your energy bills.

Electric Vehicle Charging

If you own an electric vehicle (EV), Agile Octopus can help you save money on charging costs.

Identify the periods when energy prices are lowest using our price tracker.

Many EVs come with smart charging features that allow you to schedule charging to coincide with off-peak periods. Align this with the cheapest prices.

By understanding Agile Octopus's pricing structure and implementing these smart energy strategies, you can significantly reduce your energy costs and contribute to a more sustainable future.
